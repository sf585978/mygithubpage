I live in Upstate New York and am working towards earning degrees in English Literature and Mathematics at SUNY Geneseo. In 2014, I helped start The Canonicity Project, a student led research group, with my good friend Ben Wach and our research advisor Dr. Gillian Paku. My current research involves the study of literary spaces and their manipulation. In particular, I am working on developing a theoretical framework for the study of spaces and quantitative methods for discovering them.

Over the summer of 2015, I worked as an Undergraduate Summer Research Fellow at SUNY Geneseo, developing methods for and researching how to implement a digital writing curriculum. This work will build on my experience as a writing tutor at Geneseo and hopefully be integrated into college settings, both at Geneseo and around the country.

While I may not have much of it, I spend my time outside of academics contributing to Geneseo's cross country and track and field teams. In college, my personal bests are: 25:42 for the 8k, 15:47 for the 5k, and 31:20 for the 10k. In 2013, I ran at the NCAA D3 Cross Country Championships.
