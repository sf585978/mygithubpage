SUNY Geneseo Writing Learning Center 2015 Data Report

The simplest story to tell about SUNY Geneseo's Writing Learning Center (WLC) over the last year is the story of growth. The WLC, to get right to the point, had more client interactions over the course of the 2014-2015 school year than any other in its history, having recorded 1,913 client interactions from September 2014 to May 2015. These nearly 2,000 client interactions mark a triumph success for the Writing Learning Center in moving to an online client-driven model for making appointments with writing tutors.

School Year Client Interactions
2010-2011 647
2011-2012 664
2012-2013 885
