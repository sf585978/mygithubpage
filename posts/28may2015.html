They Just Wanted to Get Laid

A Quick Read Through The Accidental Billionaires
28 May 2015

Having finished The Girl With The Dragon Tattoo the week before Memorial Day, I was looking for a new read for the long weekend. Now, the obvious choice might have been to read the sequel to TGWTDT, but I decided to alternate fiction and non-fiction works. Going into the bin of books I brought home from school, I pulled out The Accidental Billionaires, the narrative history of Facebook that insipired The Social Network. TAB had been sitting on my bookshelf for a couple of years now and it looked to be of a length that could be finished over the drive to visit family over the weekend, making it the ideal choice.
